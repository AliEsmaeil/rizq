Account Deletion

How to permanently delete your account and all associated data on EQM Truck

How to delete your account

1
Open the app and log in to your account.
2
Go to Settings → Delete Account.
3
Confirm the deletion request.

Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted, including personal information and identity verification images.

Forgot your password?

If you forgot your password, you can reset it from the app login screen. After resetting your password, log in again and follow the steps above to delete your account.
